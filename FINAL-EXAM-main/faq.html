Frequently Asked Questions


General Questions

Our tiles are made of various materials such as ceramic, porcelain, glass, and natural stones like granite and marble.

Our tiles come in various sizes and shapes, including square, rectangular, hexagonal, and mosaic.

Our tiles are available in different finishes such as glossy, matte, textured, and polished, and in a wide range of colors to suit your design needs.

Our tiles are highly durable and can withstand heavy foot traffic and wear and tear over time.

Our tiles are rated for slip-resistance to ensure safety, and we offer various options for different levels of slip-resistance depending on your needs.

Ordering

Our minimum order quantity depends on the specific tile type and size, but we typically require a minimum order of 50 to 100 square meters.

The lead time for delivery varies depending on the size and complexity of the order, but we usually deliver within 2-3 weeks from the date of order confirmation.

You can place an order by contacting us through our website or via email. We offer online shipping for all orders and can deliver to any location within the Philippines.
